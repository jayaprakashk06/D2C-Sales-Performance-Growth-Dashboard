D2C Sales Performance & Growth Dashboard
📊 Project Overview

This project presents a 3-page Power BI dashboard developed to analyze the performance of a Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) business.

The dashboard evaluates revenue trends, profitability, target achievement, customer behavior, and month-over-month growth to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard is designed to answer the following key business questions:

Are we achieving our revenue targets?

Which product categories contribute the most revenue?

Is revenue growth driven by higher order volume or increased average order value (AOV)?

How profitable is the business?

What is the repeat customer trend?

How does revenue fluctuate month-over-month?

Executive Performance Summary

🔎 Key Insights

Revenue achieved 100% of the target.

Electronics category contributes the highest share of total revenue.

Net profit margin stands at 5.6%, indicating a tight cost structure.

Revenue growth shows volatility across months, suggesting seasonal demand patterns.

Repeat customer trends indicate stable customer retention.
